Murari Chand College, Sylhet, Bangladesh.
The story is about MC College, where I finished my intermediate.

Murari Chand College, known as MC College, is one of the oldest and most historic educational institutions in Bangladesh. It was founded in 1892, making it the seventh-oldest college in the country and the very first college in the Sylhet region.

Stories

MC College Library

MC College Library is one of the oldest academic libraries in Sylhet, offering a large collection of books for HSC, Honours and Masters students. It serves as a quiet study space on campus, though many students feel it needs modernization and more updated resources.

The oldest buildings on the campus

Built between 1921-1925

Arts Building, MC College

The “Arts Building” at Murari Chand College (MC College) in Sylhet is one of the older academic blocks on campus — built in the early period after the college moved to its permanent site on Thackeray Hill (Tilagor area) in the 1920s and 1930s.

Lotus Lake (Padma Pukur)

During Rainy season, whole lake fills up with lotus. A perfect space for relaxing after study pressure.

Martyrs' Monument (Shohid Minar)

In Bangladesh, it usually refers to the monument built to honor the Language Movement martyrs of 1952.
